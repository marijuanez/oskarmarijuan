The Story
Since taking my first coding course in 2005, I have been on a continuous journey of learning and improvement, gaining valuable experience as a Web Designer, UI Designer, and Front-End Developer.

Purpose
I have over 20 years of experience in the sector, starting in 1995 in the offline world as a Layout Editor using PageMaker, QuarkXPress, and Corel during my six years at Data Color. Working as a Graphic Designer, I also acquired expertise in Final Art, collaborating closely with various printing houses.

 

 

 

 

Trajectory
In 1997, I started as a layout designer at Data Color, mastering PageMaker, QXP, Corel, Freehand, and Photoshop.

 

I spent three years at my first company, Data Color. After completing my mandatory military service, I worked for another three years at a company known as Computer to Plate.

 

In 2000, I began navigating the web and became deeply interested in the technology, prompting me to learn HTML through initial courses. Since then, I have focused on the online realm without abandoning my offline roots, often linking both in comprehensive marketing campaigns.
